COLD ROLLED ANNEALED C-45

C45 GRADE STEEL IS DEFINED AS A MEDIUM CARBON STEEL OFFERING TENSILE STRENGTHS IN THE MODEST RANGE. DUE TO THE LOW CARBON CONTENT, THIS GRADE OF STEEL OFFERS BETTER FORMING AND BENDING QUALITIES.

The material can be molded with hardening by means of quenching and tempering on focused and restricted areas. C45 can also be instigated with induction hardening up to the hardness level of HRC 55. This grade is in majority situations delivered in an unattended heat treatment state i.e. normalized condition.
However on demand it can be furnished in numerous heat treatment variations which is shown by additional letters in the name of grade. This can be furnished with minor changes in chemical composition. Machinability of C45 is equivalent to that of mild steel, on the other hand weld ability is exhibited lower than that of mild steel.
